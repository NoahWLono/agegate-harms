## Evidence readiness

The registry currently gives complete mapping coverage, not complete empirical coverage. The coverage report intentionally identifies parameters supported only by contextual evidence, official guidance, legal facts, analyst assumptions, or explicit research gaps. `--strict-evidence` is expected to fail until every model input has direct empirical support.

## Decision-use boundary

The v0.2 build is appropriate for research planning, source auditing, sensitivity analysis, assumption stress testing, reproducibility work, and identifying high-value measurements. It is not an empirical forecast of Bill 9, a health-benefit estimate, or a vendor recommendation.
