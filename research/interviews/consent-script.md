# Draft consent script

This script is a starting point and must be adapted to the applicable ethics and privacy requirements.

## Introduction

You are invited to discuss how age-assurance requirements could affect retail transactions, privacy, accessibility, costs, and lawful access. The research supports an independent, open-source policy simulator.

## Voluntary participation

Participation is voluntary. You may skip any question or stop at any time without giving a reason.

## Information collected

The interview may collect professional experience, views about implementation, and suggested evidence sources. Please do not disclose confidential employer, customer, security, or personal information that you are not authorized to share.

## Recording

The interview will not be recorded unless you separately agree. If recording is used, explain storage, access, transcription, retention, and deletion before requesting consent.

## Attribution

Choose one:

- anonymous, with no name or organization;
- role-level attribution, such as independent retailer or accessibility researcher;
- named attribution after approving the exact quotation or summary.

## Data use

Notes will be used to identify research gaps, test model assumptions, and improve documentation. Identifiable notes will not be placed in the public Git repository.

## Risks

Possible risks include discomfort discussing exclusion or surveillance, disclosure of sensitive experience, or professional concerns. Questions may be skipped.

## Contact and withdrawal

Provide the research contact, withdrawal deadline, correction process, and complaint or ethics contact where applicable.

## Consent confirmation

- I understand the purpose of the interview.
- I understand participation is voluntary.
- I understand how my information will be stored and used.
- I understand my attribution choice.
- I consent to participate.
