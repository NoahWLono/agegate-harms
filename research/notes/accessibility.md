# Accessibility research plan

## Principle

An age-assurance workflow should be tested as an end-to-end service, not only as a technically conforming screen.

## Areas to test

- keyboard and screen-reader operation;
- camera positioning and motor access;
- visual contrast and document framing;
- cognitive load, memory, transcription, and time limits;
- plain-language instructions;
- language access;
- copy and paste and password-manager support;
- CAPTCHA and liveness tasks;
- alternatives to face or document capture;
- human assistance without forced disclosure;
- appeal and recovery;
- travel and physical accessibility for in-person enrollment.

## Standards context

WCAG 2.2 Success Criterion 3.3.8 is registered as guidance for accessible authentication. It does not estimate abandonment. Test with disabled participants and accessibility specialists.

## Reporting

Report completion, time, retries, assistance, abandonment, false rejection, and qualitative burden. Separate disability-related barriers from general usability problems.
