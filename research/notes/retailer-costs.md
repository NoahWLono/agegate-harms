# Retailer-cost research plan

## Cost categories

### Fixed

- legal and compliance review;
- procurement and integration;
- devices and kiosks;
- software development;
- training and policy design;
- accessibility remediation;
- audit and certification;
- insurance and security review;
- signage and customer communication;
- vendor minimum commitments.

### Variable

- staff time per challenge;
- time per full identity proof;
- transaction fees;
- manual review;
- support contact;
- retry and appeal handling;
- queue delay;
- failed or abandoned sale;
- incident response.

## Segmentation

Estimate separately for:

- independent convenience stores;
- chains and franchises;
- supermarkets;
- pharmacies or mixed retailers if applicable;
- vending operators;
- delivery platforms;
- online sellers.

## Time-and-motion protocol

Record timestamps for greeting, challenge, document retrieval, inspection, retry, manager escalation, decision, and checkout completion. Observe peak and off-peak periods. Protect customer privacy and obtain appropriate approvals.

## Labour context

Quebec's general minimum wage is registered as $16.60 per hour effective May 1, 2026. It is a lower-bound context only. Loaded labour cost includes payroll contributions, benefits, supervision, and overhead.
