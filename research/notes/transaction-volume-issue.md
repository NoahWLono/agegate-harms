# Research task: transaction volume and buyer-age mix

Estimate annual attempted purchases of products covered by Quebec's law, segmented by product, seller type, channel, region, season, and buyer age.

## Deliverables

- transparent denominator definition;
- source and sampling frame;
- estimate and uncertainty;
- under-16 share of attempts;
- distinction between consumption, purchase, and purchase attempt;
- mapping to `population`, `purchase_attempt_rate`, and `minor_share`;
- limitations and transferability note.
