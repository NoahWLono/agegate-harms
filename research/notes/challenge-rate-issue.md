# Research task: retailer challenge rates

Measure the share of attempted transactions in which a seller requests proof under a named policy and operating context.

## Compare

- apparent-age judgment with no formal rule;
- challenge-under-21;
- challenge-under-25;
- universal ID checks;
- remote or automated workflows.

## Deliverables

Report overall and age-specific challenge rates, staff variation, store variation, time of day, training, enforcement context, and mapping to `verification_check_rate`.
