# Limitations register

This file is a standing checklist. A limitation should remain visible until evidence or a model change addresses it.

## Legal and regulatory

- Section 4 remote-sales regulations were not in force as of 2026-07-22.
- The model does not interpret due-diligence standards or enforcement discretion.
- The model does not assess constitutional validity, privacy-law compliance, or administrative-law remedies.

## Population and transactions

- The default population is normalized.
- Consumption prevalence is not transaction volume.
- The under-16 share of purchases is unknown.
- Product coverage may change by regulation.
- Seasonal, geographic, retail-channel, and product-level variation are absent.

## Behaviour

- Detection, circumvention, substitution, and proxy purchase are simplified rates.
- Informal markets and cross-border purchasing are not separate pathways.
- Repeated attempts by the same person are not modeled as a state process.
- Substitution does not estimate caffeine dose or health impact.

## Lawful access

- Adult abandonment and false rejection are not directly measured.
- The higher-friction group is aggregate.
- Stigma, dignity, anxiety, delay, and support burden are not monetized.
- Appeals are not modeled as a recovery pathway.

## Technology and privacy

- Vendor architectures differ substantially.
- A transient image can create privacy risk even when no file is retained.
- Retention is represented with a steady-state multiplier.
- Linkability, device fingerprinting, metadata, and secondary use are not fully modeled.
- Breach events may be correlated and strategically targeted.
- Identity theft and biometric harm are not equivalent per exposed record.

## Economics

- Labour ranges are illustrative.
- Queueing, lost sales, training, insurance, financing, procurement, audit, and legal costs are incomplete.
- Fixed costs are not segmented by retailer size.
- Costs to users, including time, travel, device, data, and document replacement, are not monetized.

## Uncertainty and sensitivity

- Triangular distributions are not fitted.
- Method parameters are sampled independently.
- Rank correlation captures monotonic association only.
- The priority score is a research triage heuristic.

## Health outcomes

- The model does not estimate health effects.
- Prevented transactions, prevented consumption, caffeine dose reduction, and health benefit are distinct quantities.
- Substitution may offset or reverse intended effects.
